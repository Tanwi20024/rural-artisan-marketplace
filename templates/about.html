About Us

Rural Artisan Marketplace is a platform built to help talented artisans across rural India sell their handmade products directly to customers — without relying on middlemen who often take a large share of their earnings.

India's villages are home to centuries-old traditions of pottery, handloom weaving, wood carving, bamboo craft, and more. Yet many of these artisans struggle to reach buyers beyond their local markets. Our mission is to bridge that gap using technology, giving artisans a simple way to showcase their work and giving customers access to authentic, ethically-sourced handicrafts.

Our Mission
  • Empower rural artisans with direct market access
  • Preserve and promote traditional Indian handicrafts
  • Offer customers authentic, handmade products at fair prices
  • Reduce dependency on middlemen in the handicraft supply chain
How It Works

Artisans create an account, list their products with photos and descriptions, and manage orders directly through their dashboard. Customers browse by category, place orders with Cash on Delivery, and support real craftspeople with every purchase.
