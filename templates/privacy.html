Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Rural Artisan Marketplace collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and any details you add to your profile. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address for that purpose.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process orders, manage your account, communicate order updates, and improve our platform. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database and is only accessible to authorized parts of the application, such as order processing and profile management.

Your Choices

You may update or delete your profile information at any time by visiting your Profile page. You can unsubscribe from newsletter emails by contacting us directly.

This is a student portfolio project. This policy is provided for demonstration purposes and is not a legally binding document.
